# Osaka 4-Day Itinerary (2025-05-10 to 2025-05-13)

## Day 1: Arrival & Dotonbori (2025-05-10)
- **14:00**: Arrive at Kansai International Airport (KIX).
- **16:00**: Check-in at **Hotel Nikko Osaka** (Shinsaibashi).
- **18:00**: Dinner at **Ichiran Ramen** Dotonbori.
- **20:00**: Glico Sign photo and river walk.

## Day 2: Culture & Castle (2025-05-11)
- **09:00**: Visit **Osaka Castle**.
- **12:00**: Lunch at **Kuromon Ichiba Market** (Try scallops and wagyu beef).
- **15:00**: Shinsekai district and Tsutenkaku Tower.
- **19:00**: Kushikatsu dinner.

## Day 3: Universal Studios Japan (2025-05-12)
- **08:30**: Arrive at **USJ**. 
- Highlight: Super Nintendo World & Harry Potter.
- Stay until closing.

## Day 4: Shopping & Departure (2025-05-13)
- **10:00**: Shopping at Umeda (Grand Front Osaka, LUCUA).
- **13:00**: Harukas 300 observatory.
- **16:00**: Take Haruka Express to KIX for 19:00 flight.
