# Top Restaurants & Food in Osaka

## Dotonbori (Shinsaibashi Area)
1. **Ichiran Ramen**: Famous tonkotsu ramen (Customizable).
2. **Kani Doraku**: Giant crab sign, great for grilled crab legs.
3. **Mizuno**: High-quality Okonomiyaki (Michelin recommended).

## Kuromon Ichiba Market
- **Maguroya Kurogin**: Best tuna sushi/bowls.
- **Maruzen Suisan**: Grilled scallops and sea urchin.

## Local Specialties
- **Takoyaki**: Try Kukuru or Wanaka in Dotonbori.
- **Kushikatsu**: Fried skewers (Daruma is the most famous chain).
- **551 Horai**: Pork buns (Must buy at Namba station).
